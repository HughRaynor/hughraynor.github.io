I am an NCSC accredited CHECK Team Member, CREST Registered Tester, Cyber Essentials (Plus) Assessor and GIAC GICSP, as well as being an avid reasearcher. I have strong interests in Industrial Control Systems/SCADA/Operational Technology; quantitative, technical assessments of information systems mapped to the NIST Cyber Security Framework; and Red Team scenario development and support.

I graduated from the University of Birmingham in 2018 with a BSc. in Computer Science.

Outside of cyber security, I'm an avid fan of driving and motorsport. In my spare time I volunteer as a Local Observer for the Bristol group of IAM RoadSmart, formerly the Institute of Advanced Motorists.

Should you wish to contact me, you can do so via email, Twitter, or LinkedIn.
